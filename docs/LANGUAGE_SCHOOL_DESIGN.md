# Language School - Brand Design System (2026)

## Philosophy
*   **Vibe:** Sophisticated yet approachable academic minimalism.
*   **Target Audience:** Parents and students seeking clarity, efficiency, and visible progress.
*   **UX Principles (2026):**
    *   **Bento-grid layouts** for organized information density.
    *   **Fluid micro-interactions** that provide immediate feedback.
    *   **"Aha" moments** surfaced via interactive progress indicators.

## Design Tokens

### Colors
- **Brand Primary:** `#2A5C82` (Deep Academic Blue - trustworthiness)
- **Brand Secondary:** `#FFD700` (Vibrant Academic Gold - knowledge)
- **Background:** `#F9FAFB` (Clean light neutral)
- **Text:** `#111827` (Charcoal)

### Typography
- **Display:** "Inter" Bold (700), size: 48px
- **Body:** "Inter" Regular (400), size: 16px

### Components
- **HeroSection:** High-impact with clear CTA and primary academic value prop.
- **FeatureCards:** Bento-grid cards showing curriculum highlights.
- **UserTable:** Clean overview of student progress or schedule.
